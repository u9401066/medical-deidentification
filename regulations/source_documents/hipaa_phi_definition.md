# Protected Health Information (PHI) Definition

## What is PHI?

PHI includes any information that can be used to identify an individual:

- **Direct identifiers**: Names, SSN, medical record numbers
- **Indirect identifiers**: Dates, locations, demographic information
- **Medical information**: Diagnoses, treatments, test results

## De-identification Methods

1. **Safe Harbor**: Remove 18 types of identifiers
2. **Expert Determination**: Statistical/scientific analysis

## Special Categories

### Rare Diseases
Diagnoses of rare diseases may be identifying if:
- Very small patient population
- Disease is geographically concentrated
- Combined with other demographic data
