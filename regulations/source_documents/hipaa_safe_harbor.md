# HIPAA Safe Harbor Method

## De-identification Standard

The following identifiers must be removed:

1. **Names**: All names of the individual
2. **Geographic subdivisions**: Smaller than a State
3. **Dates**: All dates directly related to an individual (except year)
4. **Telephone numbers**
5. **Fax numbers**
6. **Email addresses**
7. **Social Security numbers**
8. **Medical record numbers**
9. **Health plan beneficiary numbers**
10. **Account numbers**
11. **Ages over 89**: Must be aggregated to "90 or older"

## Special Handling for Ages

- Ages under 89: Can be retained
- Ages 89 and above: Replace with "90 or older" or age category
